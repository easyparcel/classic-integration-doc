
## Currency Conversion
Use this when your WooCommerce store currency is different from your EasyParcel currency.

<img width="2560" height="1406" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ad514964-96cd-4e86-a3f0-7ec799d201bb" />

Example:
- Store currency: USD
- EasyParcel currency: MYR

Enter how many MYR = 1 USD.
If **1 USD = 4.20 MYR**, type:

`4.20`

### Shipping Example
EasyParcel returns: **7.19 MYR**  
Conversion rate: **4.20**  
Customer pays:

`7.19 รท 4.20 = 1.71 USD`
