# Shopify Cancel Fulfillment

#### Step 1: Login to your Shopify account. And go to EasyParcel app. Go to 'Orders'. And click the order tha you would like to cancel fulfillment.
<img width="1280" height="714" alt="cancel1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a6d54462-f74e-4081-bcea-9d72c9a4c7ff" />

#### Step 2: Click the 'Cancel Fulfillment' button.
<img width="2560" height="1415" alt="cancel2" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/90f718ea-a34c-484b-9f16-7845d4abdf8e" />

#### Step 3: Fill in the cancellation reason. And click 'Yes, Cancel Fulfillment'.
<img width="2560" height="1415" alt="cancel3" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0515064a-6a88-423f-bd0b-0dd53287e89a" />

#### Step 4: After that, the order status will back to 'Unfulfilled'. 
<img width="1280" height="708" alt="cancel4" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a69dcf32-fa17-4a36-813d-8de6ea6881c7" />

#### Step 5: Scroll down, it will show the cancellation record of this order.
<img width="1280" height="713" alt="cancel5" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/eeb97edd-b93d-4625-88a8-562a41014c7a" />

#### Step 6: Some orders could not be cancelled directly in EasyParcel after clicking 'Yes, Cancel Fulfillment' at Step 3. You may contact EasyParcel support team by clicking the 'here', if you still want to proceed. And click 'Yes, Cancel on Shopify only' to cancel the orders on Shopify in order to refulfill again. If not, you can click 'No, Keep Fulfillment'.
<img width="2560" height="1422" alt="cancel6" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a30d048d-2eab-4158-a0c7-72b95484d49d" />

#### Step 7: After that, contact EasyParcel support team by clicking the 'here'. 
<img width="1280" height="710" alt="cancel7" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d0f62031-fb85-4df5-a71c-50c32f27a408" />

#### Step 8: It will redirect you to EayParcel portal. Select 'Shipment Enquiry'.
<img width="1280" height="710" alt="cancel8" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0f45a1d0-fa45-437b-97d0-c0ceb3c4fa46" />

#### Step 9: Select 'Domestic Shipment'.
<img width="2560" height="1422" alt="cancel9" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6a9326d8-94d9-44ec-840d-53145d6cd769" />

#### Sep 10: Then, select 'Parcel Cancellation'.
<img width="1280" height="712" alt="cancel10" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3961bd3c-783f-4a37-8752-0014f74326a3" />

#### Step 11: Then, fill in the required information and click 'Submit Now'.
<img width="2560" height="1418" alt="cancel11" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6b7929a5-b14a-40c2-8c0a-b1955fcab6cd" />
