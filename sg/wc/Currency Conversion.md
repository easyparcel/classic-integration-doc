## Currency Conversion

Use this setting when your **WooCommerce store currency** is different from your **EasyParcel currency**.

Example:
- Store currency: USD
- EasyParcel currency: MYR

### What to enter?
Enter how many MYR = 1 USD.

Example:
If **1 USD = 4.20 MYR**, enter:
`4.20`

---

### Example 1 — Shipping Price
- EasyParcel returns: **7.19 MYR**
- Conversion rate: **4.20**

Customer will pay:
`7.19 ÷ 4.20 = 1.71 USD`
